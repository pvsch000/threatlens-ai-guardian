# 🛡️ PVS ThreatLens

## See the Threat Before It Sees You

An AI-Powered Cybersecurity Intelligence Platform that helps users detect phishing emails, scam SMS messages, fake job offers, malicious URLs, banking fraud attempts, and social engineering attacks before they become victims.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Every day, millions of people receive:

* Phishing Emails
* Scam SMS Messages
* Fake Job Offers
* Banking Fraud Messages
* Suspicious URLs
* Social Engineering Attacks

Most users cannot accurately determine whether a message is safe or malicious.

PVS ThreatLens uses Artificial Intelligence to analyze threats, explain risks, simulate attack chains, and educate users before they become victims.

---

## 🚀 Why PVS ThreatLens?

Traditional phishing detectors only tell users:

❌ Safe

❌ Unsafe

PVS ThreatLens goes further.

It acts as an AI Security Analyst that explains:

* Why the content is dangerous
* Which social engineering tactics are being used
* Who is likely to be targeted
* What could happen if the victim interacts with the threat
* How to stay protected
